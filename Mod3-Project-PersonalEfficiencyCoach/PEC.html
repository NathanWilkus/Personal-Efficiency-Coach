What To Do

Fill out what each day should look like in terms of the tasks and goals most important to you, with a set time allocation. After that task is completed, you select how well you personally think you accomplished that task or goal. At the end of the day, you'll receive an overall efficiency rating. Be sure to schedule in some leisure, but when it's time to be disciplined, than be disciplined, and be amazed at what you can achieve. Links to videos, apps and other tools can be placed right in the description field when filling out your day. Now go and live the best version of yourself.
